ABOUT US

Sai Pragathi Sumedha Commercial offers a refreshing escape from the chaos of urban life. We create spaces where nature and thoughtful design come together, providing clean air, tranquility, and harmony. Our vision is to foster well-being and offer a balanced lifestyle, free from the pollution and noise of daily life. Experience the difference of living in a space that truly cares for you.

SALIENT FEATURES

Sai Pragathi Sumedha Commercial is a perfect blend of modern design and natural harmony, located on the main road at Boduppal Junction near Uppal Depot. With G+5 floors of thoughtfully crafted commercial space, ample parking, and excellent connectivity, it offers the ideal environment for growth and success. Surrounded by refreshing greenery and clean air, it creates a serene atmosphere where businesses and communities can thrive, combining convenience with a commitment to well-being and sustainability.
