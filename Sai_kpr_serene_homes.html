Sai KPR Serene Homes

ABOUT US

Sai KPR Serene Homes is designed to be your peaceful retreat amidst the hustle and bustle of city life. We blend modern living with serene environments, creating a haven where you can relax, rejuvenate, and thrive. Our commitment is to provide thoughtfully planned spaces that prioritize comfort, convenience, and a connection with nature. Discover a lifestyle that’s harmonious, refreshing, and truly serene, where your well-being comes first. Experience the joy of living in a space that feels like home in every way.

SALIENT FEATURES

Sai KPR Serene Homes offers an HMDA-approved layout with 30', 40', and 60' blacktop roads, underground electricity, a waterline, and drainage systems. The project features a grand entrance arch, avenue plantation, parks, children's play areas, and 24x7 security. With 100% Vastu compliance, spot registration, and proximity to top schools, colleges, and major hubs like AIIMS, Infosys, and the Outer Ring Road, it ensures a harmonious and well-connected lifestyle.

SPECIFICATIONS

Superframework/Structure:

RCC Frame Structure Constructed with Fe 550 Grade Steel approved branding Cement approved brand by the Structural Engineer of 53 Grade for Structure.43 Grade for brick work.

Painting:

Exterior ace for External walls & luppam Finish for all internal walls with Tractor Emulsion Paint for inernal walls, Enamel paint for Doors and Door Frames. Main Door finish with Melamine Polish.

Kitchen:

Granite Base Platform, Black granite top with built in Single Bowl Stainless SteelSink and 2'-0" Heigh Ceramic tiles for dadooing. Two steel taps., One Aqua Guard Point, Exhaust hole.

Sanitary

One Indian type & One Floor mountated White WC or Commode with low level P.V.C. system in each toilet. One C.P. Shower and wall mixture of standard make (IS.L.) in each bath room (Make Jaguar or equivalent Wash basin in Dinning area and toilets. (White) Two steel taps. (Jaguar or Equivalent) Geyser point. All Water Pipe Line ISI Make CPVC pipes. All Sewerage Line of PVC of ISI (Nandi or Prince Make] And Spouts up to Ground level Lines connecting to man holes of 4" Diameter (SWG) pipe. Borewell providing, Borewell to Over Head Tank connection.

Superframework :

All External walls will be 6" thick in Cement Mortar with AAC bricks. All internal walls will be 4" thick in Cement Mortar with AAC bricks.

Main Door :

Main doors with Best Quality Teak Frame Section of 3-6"x7"- Size and Panel of Flush Shutter of 37 mm thick with Flegant Design, Polish and Necessry Hardware Fitting, All Internal doors with Medium Teak wood Frame Section of 7' 00 Height and Panel of Flush shutter with Water proof door shutters providing with chromium plated steel except Bathroom.
