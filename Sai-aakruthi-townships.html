ABOUT US

Aakruthi Township is more than just a place to live—it's a breath of fresh air that invites you to experience life in harmony with nature. In today’s world, where overcrowded spaces and the constant noise of urban life often leave little room for peace, we believe that true well-being comes from living in an environment that nurtures the body, mind, and soul. Aakruthi Township offers an escape from the chaos, where open spaces, clean air, and natural beauty foster a sense of calm and rejuvenation. Here, you’ll find a community designed not only for comfort but for a lifestyle that embraces nature’s refreshing touch, ensuring every day feels like a retreat.

SALIENT FEATURES

Aakruthi Township is where modern living meets the serenity of nature. With a focus on eco-friendly design, top-notch amenities, and a safe, secure setting, Aakruthi Township is the perfect place to call home. Here, residents thrive in harmony with nature while enjoying the conveniences of modern life. Join us and experience a lifestyle that is both enriching and truly refreshing.

MASTER PLAN

Aakruthi Township blends modern design with nature’s tranquility, offering luxurious living spaces amidst 5 acres of lush greenery. Featuring nine 8-storey towers, the township offers designer units ranging from 845 sq.ft to 1945 sq.ft, equipped with top-notch amenities for comfort and luxury. With its unique style, refined sophistication, and spacious layouts, Aakruthi Township is the perfect place for families to live their dream in an environment that defines their ideal lifestyle.

SPECIFICATIONS

Structure:

R.C.C. framed structure designed to withstand wind and seismic loads. First-class brick masonry in cement mortar.

Plastering:

Internal smooth lime finish, external double coat sand-faced cement plastering.

Joinery:

Teak wood main doors with designer hardware; internal doors with wood frames; anodized aluminum frames for windows and French doors with glass panels.

Flooring:

Vitrified tiles in living, drawing, and bedrooms; anti-skid ceramic tiles in bathrooms and utility areas; designer tiles in kitchens.

Cladding & Dadoing:

Designer tiles in bathrooms and kitchen; granite platform with stainless steel sink in the kitchen; marble/granite flooring and lift cladding.

Electrical:

Concealed copper wiring, power outlets for air conditioning and geysers; MCB and ECLB for safety; high-quality electrical fittings.

Painting:

Exterior rendered with Apex emulsion; interior with premier emulsion paint.

Lifts:

Standard make lifts provided.

Communication:

Telephone and cable TV points in all bedrooms and living rooms.

Toilets:

High-quality EWC with flush tanks, geyser provisions, and reputed CP fittings.

Water Supply & Plumbing:

ISI-mark GI/PPR/CPVC piping; PVC sanitary piping; professional installation.

Fire Safety:

Fire detection and fighting systems as per norms.
